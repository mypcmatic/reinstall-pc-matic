How to Reinstall PC Matic by Device

Follow these detailed instructions based on your device type to properly reinstall PC Matic.

Note: Before reinstalling, ensure you have your PC Matic account credentials and license information available.

1

Reinstalling on Windows

  1. Uninstall PC Matic:
    1. Open Windows Settings and go to "Apps"
    2. Locate PC Matic in the installed apps list
    3. Click "Uninstall" and follow the prompts
    4. Restart your computer when complete
  2. Download the Installer:
    1. Log in to your PC Matic account
    2. Navigate to the downloads section
    3. Download the latest version for Windows
  3. Install PC Matic:
    1. Run the downloaded installer file
    2. Follow the on-screen installation prompts
    3. Enter your account credentials when prompted
    4. Complete the initial setup and run a quick scan
2

Reinstalling on Mac

  1. Uninstall PC Matic:
    1. Open Finder and go to Applications
    2. Drag PC Matic to the Trash
    3. Empty the Trash to complete removal
    4. Restart your Mac
  2. Download the Installer:
    1. Log in to your PC Matic account
    2. Access the Mac download section
    3. Download the latest DMG file
  3. Install PC Matic:
    1. Open the downloaded DMG file
    2. Drag PC Matic to your Applications folder
    3. Launch PC Matic from Applications
    4. Sign in with your account credentials
    5. Complete the initial configuration
3

Reinstalling on Mobile Devices

  1. Uninstall the App:
    1. Press and hold the PC Matic app icon
    2. Select "Uninstall" (Android) or tap the "X" (iOS)
    3. Confirm the uninstallation
    4. Restart your device
  2. Reinstall the App:
    1. Open the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android)
    2. Search for "PC Matic"
    3. Download and install the official app
  3. Set Up the App:
    1. Open the newly installed app
    2. Log in with your PC Matic credentials
    3. Complete any device verification steps

Troubleshooting Reinstallation Issues

If you encounter problems during the reinstallation process, try these solutions:

  3. Check that your system meets minimum requirements
  4. Try downloading the installer again in case of corruption

  1. Use the official PC Matic uninstall tool if available
  2. Try uninstalling in Windows Safe Mode
  3. Check for any running PC Matic processes in Task Manager
  4. Use a third-party uninstaller as last resort

Activation Problems

  1. Verify you're using the correct account credentials
  2. Check your subscription status in your online account
  3. Ensure your license hasn't reached its device limit
  4. Try activating on a different network connection

Performance Issues After Reinstall

  1. Allow the initial scan and updates to complete
  2. Check for conflicting software
  3. Review your scan schedules and settings
  4. Verify your system resources aren't being maxed out
